# What are the benefits of this integration

<v-clicks>

* Complex requests where you want smaller checkpoints and review points.
* Work that needs planning before execution (scope, priorities, clear “done” criteria).
* Multi-step or multi-agent flows where dependencies control what starts when.
* When you want to avoid duplicate effort by searching existing tasks first.
* Draft-first planning: create and refine tasks until they look right, then execute.
* When handoffs matter (another agent/session can continue from task context).
* When feedback and outcomes should be captured (notes, decisions, next steps).

</v-clicks>
