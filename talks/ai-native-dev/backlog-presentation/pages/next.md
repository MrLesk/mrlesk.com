<h1 class="pt-4">What's next?</h1>

<v-clicks>

* Agile Software Development must adapt to AI-native workflows
* AI Agents will become first-class citizens in our teams
* Story points estimations will be obsolete due to almost infinite capacity
* We need to look at the core principles: knowing what to build, customer collaboration, responding to change

</v-clicks>

<h3 v-click class=mt-10 mb-4>The challenges</h3>

<v-clicks >

* **Automated review:** do you trust AI Agents to merge code?
* **Conflict resolution:** how many tasks can we have in progress simultaneously?
* **Humans in the loop:** verify deliverables and steer the development towards success

</v-clicks>
