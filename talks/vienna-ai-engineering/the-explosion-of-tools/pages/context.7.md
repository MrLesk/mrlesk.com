# Protocols

There is an explosion of protocols, each claiming to be the standard

<v-clicks>

- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**: popular protocol for adding tools, resources and prompts to AI Agents
- **ACP (Agent Communication Protocol)**: enables communication between AI Agents, applications and humans
- **A2A (Agent to Agent)**: Similar to ACP but with centralized state management for complex interactions
- **Google UCP (Universal Commerce Protocol) / OpenAI ACP (Agentic Commerce Protocol)**: AI-led shopping

</v-clicks>
